How to make a milkshake

step1:

soften the icecream and chill the glass.Take the icecream out of yhe freezer for 10 to 15 minutes before use...

step2:

measure the ingrediants.1 cup icecream to 1/4 cup milk is great ratio

step3:

blend the shake .In a blender,combine the milk and icecream and blend until smooth...

your milkshake is ready to serve
